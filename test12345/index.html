MEISNER TECHNIQUE

The Meisner Technique empowers actors to get out of their heads and access a profound sense of truth in their work by being present and listening.

The technique employs practical and progressive exercises that train actors to respond truthfully in the moment, liberating them from self-consciousness. At its core is the Repetition Exercise, which teaches actors to observe precisely and respond instinctively to others' behavior rather than fixating on text.

My classes offer a supportive environment where you can take risks, embrace vulnerability, and develop the authenticity that defines exceptional acting.

ABOUT ME

I have been teaching the Meisner technique since 2018 at prestigious institutions including Royal Central School of Speech and Drama, Giles Foreman Centre for Acting, Fourth Monkey, and The Impulse Company. My work extends to bespoke training programmes, private coaching, and accessible drop-in classes.

It is of utmost importance to me that I create a safe, encouraging learning environment where students can identify and overcome their artistic barriers. My teaching emphasizes patience, self-compassion, and ownership of one's journey as an actor.
